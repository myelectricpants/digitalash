We had a house concert here the other day. A long time friend of mine came who has been doing a band and solo work for a long time. He brought along a guy who was a bit more famous, having been in a somewhat bigger band for a number of years before going solo.

I felt a lot of [[envy]] and [[jealousy]] while they were here. In part because they are working musicians, but also that I have never given any of it much of a chance. I felt like it was pointless and gave up on it at an early age. Even though [[performing]] is one of my favorite things. [[Music]] was always so important to me, still is, and I spend so much time listening, but very little time creating anymore.

Would I even want to be doing what they were doing? I'm not sure about that part, because its not even what I envisioned for myself. That's what makes the envy so weird. But its a really persistent feeling whenever I'm around musicians. Here's people doing what I could have been doing. Or still could be doing, and yet some deep part of me must not want to.

It's like so many desires that I have. They are both present and wanted, and yet undesirable at the same time.
